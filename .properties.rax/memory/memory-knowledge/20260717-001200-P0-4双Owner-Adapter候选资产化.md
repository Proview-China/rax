## 候选冻结点

- 两Owner Adapter只读未来各自V2 Reader，只封装Owner exact refs/digests；不复制Projection/Item/current，不建第二Store/Pointer/CAS。
- 每侧独立冻结StableAssociation canonical body和opaque fresh Envelope；stable排除Inspection/Projection/Observation fresh refs、Binding current digests、phase/time/expiry，Envelope包含这些fresh字段但不含`Current`或body。
- 每次S1/S2对Owner Adapter、Application Coordinator、Context Adapter和Generation association执行Binding前后复读；TTL取所有Owner/Runtime/Context上界最小值。
- S1/S2 fresh refs允许不同，StableAssociationDigest与Owner StableClosure/exact集合必须相同。
- 零Provider、网络、Resolver和远程fallback；失败返回Owner closed error、零Envelope/零body。
